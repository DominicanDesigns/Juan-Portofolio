Apps Details

When you purchase an app or product from me, I manage the end-to-end process so you get a polished, tested product ready for users. I cover planning, design, development, testing, deployment and initial support — all tailored to your project's needs.

What I normally do after a purchase

  • Requirements & planning — clarify features, target platforms, and delivery milestones.
  • Design & prototyping — wireframes and UI mockups with feedback rounds.
  • Implementation — development of the app, integrations, and content population as agreed.
  • Testing — unit, integration, and user acceptance testing across devices.
  • Deployment — publish to stores or set up hosting/CI, plus domain/SSL when needed.
  • Handover & docs — delivery of source, credentials, and brief training/documentation.
  • Post-launch support — 30 days of support included; ongoing maintenance plans are available.

Typical timeline: 3–12 weeks depending on complexity. For a tailored quote and timeline, contact me.
